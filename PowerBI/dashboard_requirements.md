# Power BI Dashboard Requirements

## Objective

The Power BI dashboard provides business insights for the Real-Time Customer Engagement Analytics Platform built using Microsoft Fabric.

## Dashboard Pages

### Page 1 – Executive Summary
- Total Customers
- Total Campaign Revenue
- Total Tickets
- Average Resolution Days
- Customer Engagement Distribution
- Customers by Loyalty Tier
- Country Filter
- Registration Date Filter

### Page 2 – Campaign Performance
- Campaign Revenue
- Campaign Clicks
- Total Purchases
- Average Conversion Rate
- Revenue by Campaign
- Campaign Clicks Analysis
- Purchases by Channel
- Revenue by Channel

### Page 3 – Support Analytics
- Total Tickets
- Open Tickets
- Closed Tickets
- Average Resolution Days
- Tickets by Priority
- Tickets by Issue Type
- Open vs Closed Ticket Analysis

## Business KPIs
- Customer Engagement
- Campaign Effectiveness
- Customer Acquisition
- Campaign Revenue
- Support Performance
- Ticket Resolution Efficiency

## Technologies Used
- Microsoft Power BI Desktop
- Microsoft Fabric Warehouse
- Semantic Model
- DAX Measures
- Interactive Visualizations
- Slicers and Filters

## Outcome
The dashboard enables business users to monitor customer engagement, evaluate campaign performance, analyze support operations, and make data-driven decisions through interactive visualizations.
