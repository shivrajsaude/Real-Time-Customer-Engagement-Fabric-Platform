# Business Problem

Modern organizations interact with customers through multiple digital channels such as websites, mobile applications, marketing campaigns, and customer support systems. These systems generate large volumes of customer engagement data that are often stored in separate silos.

Business stakeholders require a unified analytics platform capable of:

- Monitoring customer engagement in near real-time
- Measuring campaign performance
- Tracking customer support efficiency
- Identifying highly engaged customers
- Supporting data-driven marketing decisions

The objective of this project is to build an end-to-end Real-Time Customer Engagement Analytics Platform using Microsoft Fabric that centralizes customer engagement data and delivers business-ready insights through Medallion Architecture.
